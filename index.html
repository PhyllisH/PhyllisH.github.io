Biography

I am a postdoctoral fellow at University of Michigan in the Department of Robotics, working with Prof. Maani Ghaffari, Prof. Henry Liu, and Prof. Ram Vasudevan. I obtained my Ph. D. degree in Shanghai Jiao Tong Universiy under the supervision of Prof. Siheng Chen. My primary research interest is robotic perception and navigation, and multi-agent collaboration.

I am actively looking for jobs and collaboration opportunities. Do not hesitate to drop me an email if you are interested :)
